Othello

Mar 26-Apr 18, Fri Mar 26 & Sat Mar 27,
Apr 3,10 & 17 at 8PM; Sun Mar 28, Apr 11&18 at 3PM

African-American Shakespeare Company

Buriel Clay Memorial Theater

$30; $20 seniors & students; Special Early Bird Ticket price of $10 ends on March 13th
Description

Told simultaneously from three (Othello, Desdemona, and Iago) points of view, this compelling story re-sets one of Shakespeare's greatest tragedies in a contemporary military regime, where race, class, and sexual politics become the driving force for jealousy and revenge.
